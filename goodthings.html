Our Big Question: Is the silk a story of peaceful trade, and a rich history of religious and harmonious cultural exchange?

The Silk Road is a peaceful trade, and a rich history of religious and harmonious cultural exchange for
several reasons. First of all, it helped to generate trade and commerce between a number of different
kingdoms and empires. As the Silk Road grew, more people started working on the Silk Road and started
making money. The Silk Road also helped the spread of religions. Before the Silk Road, Buddhism was
about to die out. However, the Silk Road helped to spread Buddhism, so it saved the religion from dying
out.
